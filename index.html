awesome for users

  • Integrated with reddit.com

    No need to download applications or switching between windows, OrangeChat sits directly inside reddit.com while your reading posts so you don't miss a message. Discuss the post you're reading or talk with like-minded people in your existing communities.

  • Create your own channels

    Sometimes you want to talk in your own group of friends or create your own circles. Create and invite people to your own channel with your own rules. Start your own community!

  • Take it anywhere with you

    Not always browsing reddit.com? Use our webapp or even the mobile apps anywhere you go.

  • Already knows your subscribed subreddits

    You can dive right in. There is no need to go hunting for your existing communities as they are all one click away to get involved.

awesome for mods

  • You're in charge

    Subreddit channels automatically know that you're a moderator. You can manage your channels to remove the trouble makers and keep the friendly atmosphere going.

  • Easy to use

    No commands to remember and no prior knowledge required. We're trying to keep it simple - no one should need a manual to moderate a chatroom.

  • Moderator only channels

    Running a subreddit with many mods needs a place for mod-only discussion. Plan, discuss and keep everyone in the loop with a channel without distractions.

  • IRC integration

    Does your subreddit already have an IRC channel? Link your OrangeChat channel with IRC so that your subscribers can join directly from reddit.com without the need to learn anything IRC specific. Much more friendlier and easier to get everyone involved.
